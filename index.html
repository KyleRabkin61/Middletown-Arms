About Us

Middletown Arms is a trusted, family-owned firearms retailer and licensed manufacturer. We proudly serve the firearms community with integrity and expertise, offering a full range of services for both new and experienced gun owners. Our team includes certified instructors and retired law enforcement professionals who bring real-world training and insight. At Middletown Arms, we are committed to educating, equipping, and empowering customers while promoting safe, responsible gun ownership.

Meet the Owners

Amanda Malone grew up in Middletown, New Jersey, where she was introduced to firearms at a young age. From the very beginning, she learned the importance of firearm safety and proper handling. This early exposure sparked a lifelong interest in firearms, and over the years, Amanda has shared her knowledge and commitment to safety with friends and family. Amanda is proud to be the first woman to own a gun store and is dedicated to serving her community.

Vincent Malone, Amanda's husband, was raised in Staten Island, New York. A Glock and SIG Sauer Certified Armorer, Vincent is also a certified firearms instructor. Like Amanda, Vincent was introduced to firearms early in life attending shooting events and going on hunting trips with his family. He has passed his passion and knowledge down to their two children and provides youth safety instruction and basic firearm introduction to new shooters.
