About Us

Middletown Arms is proud to be both a trusted retailer and a licensed manufacturer of firearms. As a family-owned and operated business, we are committed to serving the firearms community with integrity, professionalism, and expertise. We offer a full range of services so whether you're a first-time gun owner or a seasoned enthusiast, our team is here to support you every step of the way. Our store is a true one-stop shop for all your firearms needs. Our knowledgeable staff includes certified firearms instructors with extensive backgrounds in law enforcement. Many of our team members are retired police instructors with experience at the local, county, and state levels, bringing a wealth of real-world training and insight to our customers. At Middletown Arms, we take pride in educating, equipping, and empowering our customers while promoting safe, responsible gun ownership.
